Professional Biography

Khandaker Ashraful Islam is a dedicated writer at Trimatri Media Agency, where he contributes his creative skills and storytelling expertise to various projects. With his keen interest in creating compelling narratives, he plays a key role in creating content that communicates ideas with clarity, emotion, and impact. His work reflects both creativity and professionalism, making him an integral part of the agency's mission to provide high-quality media and communication solutions.

His documentaries "Andhar Periye" and "Nari" have received great appreciation both in the country and abroad. Khandaker Ashraful Islam has worked in various national and international NGOs for over 31 years in mid-to-high management positions. He has also written novels, poems and play scripts. His novels - Borkha, Goa-Kashi-Vrindavan and books of poetry - Troye Ninanda and Saree Bhalo Rathna Hola.
