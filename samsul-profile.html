Professional Biography

Since childhood, Md. Shamsul Alam had a great interest in creative works like writing, acting, fine arts, painting, etc. After completing a Diploma in Computer Science and Hardware Management in 1995, he started his career in the IT sector. In 1998, he completed his graduation and transitioned into Television Media as a Video Editor.

Throughout his long career, he has worked with renowned personalities like Afzal Hossain, Mr. Subhash Dutt, and Mr. Kazi Hayat. He obtained his master's degree from National University in 2001. As a Senior Video Editor, he has worked for major private televisions like Channel-I, Diganta TV, and RTV. He has more than 1500 dramas, telefilms, documentaries, and reality shows to his credit.

He has produced content for various government and international bodies including the Ministry of Education, Bangladesh Bank, A2i, JIKA, and Save the Children. Notably, his work as a Chief Video Editor on the biographical documentary "Political Life of Bangabandhu and Emergence of Bangladesh" was awarded Best Documentary Film 2020.

As a writer, his notable works include drama scripts and novels like Ridoy Kuthire, Nishideep, and Bristy O Valobashar Golpo. With nearly 23 years of vast experience, he founded Trimatri Media Agency on January 1, 2025, where he currently serves as the Creative Director.
