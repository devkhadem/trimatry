Professional Biography

H M Noor serves as the Art Director at Trimatri Media Agency, where he leads with vision and creativity in shaping the visual identity of diverse projects. With a keen eye for design and a deep understanding of visual storytelling, he oversees the artistic direction that defines the agency's work.

His expertise lies in combining innovation with aesthetic precision, ensuring that each project reflects both originality and professional excellence. Under his direction, visual concepts are transformed into impactful designs that engage audiences and enhance brand communication.

H M Noor has completed his masters from National University of Bangladesh in 2016. He worked as a Chief Assistant Director with many renowned film makers and directors for making many Film & Short Films, TV Dramas, Documentary Films on many National and Multinational organizations like CDD, Walton, JICA, and BBC Bangla as cinematographer. He is a member of Dhaka Theater form 2003. He has already proved his work in many local televisions like, Bangla Vision, Diganta TV, etc.
